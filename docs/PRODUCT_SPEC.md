# Product Spec - La Mesa MVP

## Overview

La Mesa is the ISD Founder 12 cohort portal for Costa Rica.

It helps founders apply for Founder 12, helps ISD evaluate and select 12 accepted founders, and gives accepted founders a coordinated space for Founders Coffee, ISD member privileges, AI sessions, team formation support, and prototype readiness.

Builder matching is part of the support model, but La Mesa is not primarily a marketplace.

## Product Positioning

La Mesa is the digital front door and operating layer for Founder 12.

It should feel like a premium, selective, founder-centered ISD cohort experience.

## Core Users

### Founder 12 Applicant

A founder in Costa Rica or connected to the ISD ecosystem who wants to apply for a seat in Founder 12.

### Accepted Founder

One of the 12 founders selected by ISD. Accepted founders unlock Founders Coffee coordination, ISD member privileges, AI sessions, team formation support, and roadmap visibility.

### Builder

An approved member of the Builder Network who may be matched to accepted Founder 12 ventures based on skills, availability, interests, and fit.

### Admin

An ISD or Mindful Tech operator who reviews applications, selects the 12 founders, approves builders, coordinates cohort support, and manages team formation.

## MVP Problem

ISD needs a clean way to collect serious founder applications, evaluate readiness, select the right 12 founders, and coordinate what happens after acceptance.

Founders need clarity on:
- Whether they are under review.
- Whether they were accepted.
- What Founder 12 unlocks.
- What they need to do next.
- How ISD will help them move toward prototype readiness.

Builders need a trusted path to support serious Founder 12 ventures without the noise of an open marketplace.

## MVP Solution

La Mesa provides:
- Founder 12 public landing page.
- Structured Founder 12 application.
- Structured Builder Network profile.
- AI founder readiness scoring.
- AI role mapping.
- AI 30-day milestone roadmap.
- Admin application review and selection.
- Accepted-founder dashboard.
- Founders Coffee coordination surface.
- ISD member privilege surface.
- AI session surface.
- Admin-assisted builder matching.
- Simple venture tracking.

## Key Product Promise

La Mesa helps a founder move from:

**Application -> ISD Review -> Founder 12 Acceptance -> Founders Coffee -> AI Sessions -> Team Formation -> Prototype Readiness**

## MVP Success Metric

The MVP succeeds if ISD can select and coordinate the first Founder 12 cohort.

Primary pilot metric:
- 12 accepted Founder 12 members selected and onboarded.

Secondary pilot metrics:
- Number of applications submitted.
- Number of applications reviewed.
- Number of founders shortlisted.
- Number of founders accepted.
- Number of accepted founders attending Founders Coffee.
- Number of accepted founders using AI sessions.
- Number of accepted ventures receiving builder support.
- Number of ventures reaching prototype-ready status.
- Number of ventures presented at demo day or founder showcase.

## Public Pages

### Home

Purpose: Explain Founder 12 and drive founder applications.

### How It Works

Purpose: Explain application, ISD review, acceptance, Founders Coffee, AI sessions, and team formation.

### Apply

Purpose: Route founders into the Founder 12 application flow.

### Join

Purpose: Route builders into the Builder Network profile flow.

### Login

Purpose: Allow returning applicants, accepted founders, builders, and admins to access the app.

## App Pages

### Founder Portal

Shows application status, AI analysis, next steps, and accepted-founder access if selected.

### Founder 12 Application

Guided selective cohort application form.

### Venture Detail Page

Shows venture overview, AI analysis, readiness score, role map, roadmap, team status, admin notes, and accepted-founder next steps.

### Accepted Founder Area

For accepted founders only.

MVP modules may include:
- Founders Coffee details.
- ISD member privileges.
- AI session information.
- Community chat external link.
- Team formation status.

### Builder Profile Page

Allows builders to create or update their Builder Network profile.

### Builder Project View

For MVP, builders should only see assigned Founder 12 ventures.

### Admin Dashboard

Cohort Command Center for reviewing applications, tracking the 12-seat cohort, approving builders, and managing team formation.

### Admin Applications / Ventures

Allows admins to review, shortlist, accept, reject, and manage founder applications.

### Admin Builders

Allows admins to review and approve builders.

### Admin Matching

Allows admins to assign builders to accepted Founder 12 ventures.

## MVP Constraints

The MVP should avoid:
- Public marketplace behavior.
- Public project browsing.
- Native chat.
- Full calendar scheduling.
- Full membership CRM.
- Automated founder acceptance.
- Automated builder assignment.
- Payment processing.
- Legal/equity handling.
- Mentor management.
- Investor dashboards.
- University partner dashboards.

## Product Tone

Clear, warm, selective, and founder-centered.

Use direct language:
- Apply for Founder 12.
- Your application is under ISD review.
- You have been accepted to Founder 12.
- Founders Coffee access unlocked.
- ISD member privileges.
- AI session access.
- Team formation support.
- Move from proof of concept to prototype readiness.

Avoid:
- Generic marketplace language.
- Overly academic program language.
- Startup theater.
- Jargon soup.
