# Product Spec — La Mesa MVP

## Overview

La Mesa is an AI-assisted startup incubation and team-formation platform. It helps founders submit ideas, helps builders submit profiles, uses AI to map project needs, and enables admins to form teams manually with AI support.

## Product Positioning

La Mesa helps founders, students, and builders bring ideas to the table, find the right collaborators, and move from concept to prototype.

## Core Users

### Founder
A person with a project or startup idea who needs help forming a team.

### Builder
A collaborator who wants to join a startup/project team based on their skills, interests, and availability.

### Admin
An ISD or Mindful Tech operator who reviews submissions, approves profiles, and manages team formation.

## MVP Problem

Founders and students often have ideas but struggle to find reliable collaborators with the right skills, availability, and interest.

Universities and startup communities often have talent, but that talent is scattered and underutilized.

## MVP Solution

La Mesa provides:
- Structured project intake
- Structured builder profiles
- AI role mapping
- AI readiness scoring
- AI milestone roadmaps
- Admin-assisted team matching
- Simple project tracking

## Key Product Promise

La Mesa helps a project move from:

**Idea → Role Map → Team Match → 30-Day Roadmap → Prototype Progress**

## MVP Success Metric

The MVP succeeds if ISD can form project teams and move them toward prototype completion.

Primary pilot metric:
- Number of teams formed

Secondary pilot metrics:
- Number of projects submitted
- Number of builders submitted
- Number of projects approved
- Number of teams actively building
- Number of prototypes completed
- Number of projects presented at demo day

## Public Pages

### Home
Purpose: Explain La Mesa and drive founder/builder applications.

### How It Works
Purpose: Explain the idea-to-team-to-prototype flow.

### Apply as Founder
Purpose: Route users into founder onboarding.

### Join as Builder
Purpose: Route users into builder onboarding.

### Login
Purpose: Allow returning users to access the app.

## App Pages

### Founder Dashboard
Shows submitted projects and status.

### New Project Application
Guided startup application form.

### Project Detail Page
Shows project overview, AI analysis, team, role gaps, and roadmap.

### Builder Profile Page
Allows builders to create or update their profile.

### Builder Project View
For MVP, builders should only see assigned projects.

### Admin Dashboard
Shows key operating metrics and pending work.

### Admin Projects
Allows admins to review and manage projects.

### Admin Builders
Allows admins to review and approve builders.

### Admin Matching
Allows admins to assign builders to projects.

## MVP Constraints

The MVP should avoid:
- Open marketplace behavior
- Public project browsing
- Automated team assignment
- Real-time chat
- Complex permissions
- Payment processing
- Legal/equity handling
- Mentor management
- University partner dashboards

## Product Tone

Clear, warm, and founder-friendly.

Avoid corporate jargon.

Use direct language:
- Bring your idea to the table.
- Find the people your idea needs.
- Move from idea to prototype.
- Build with the right team.
