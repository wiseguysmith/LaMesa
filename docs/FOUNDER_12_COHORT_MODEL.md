# Founder 12 Cohort Model

## Purpose

Founder 12 is the center of La Mesa.

La Mesa exists to help ISD run a selective founder cohort in Costa Rica. Founders apply, ISD selects 12, and accepted founders receive a focused operating experience: Founders Coffee coordination, ISD member privileges, AI sessions, admin-guided builder matching, and prototype readiness support.

Builder matching is a benefit of the cohort. It is not the primary product identity.

## Core Promise

Founder 12 helps a founder move from:

Application -> ISD Review -> Acceptance -> Founders Coffee -> AI Sessions -> Team Formation -> Prototype Readiness

## Who Gets Access

### Public Visitors

Can:
- Learn about Founder 12.
- Apply as a founder.
- Join the Builder Network.
- Log in.

Cannot:
- View founder applications.
- View accepted-founder content.
- View builder matching data.

### Founder 12 Applicants

Can:
- Submit a Founder 12 application.
- View their application status.
- View AI readiness analysis and roadmap when available.
- Receive next steps from ISD.

Cannot:
- Access Founders Coffee details unless accepted.
- Access ISD member privileges unless accepted.
- Access AI session details unless accepted.
- Browse builders.
- Self-assign builders.

### Accepted Founders

Accepted founders are the 12 selected members of Founder 12.

Can:
- View accepted-founder dashboard.
- View Founders Coffee details and RSVP/status.
- View ISD member privileges.
- View AI session access/details.
- View team formation status.
- View assigned builders.
- View 30-day roadmap and progress updates.

### Builders

Builders apply to the La Mesa Builder Network.

Can:
- Submit builder profile.
- View profile approval status.
- View assigned Founder 12 ventures after assignment.

Cannot:
- Browse all ventures.
- Contact founders through native chat.
- Assign themselves to ventures.

### Admins

Admins operate the cohort.

Can:
- Review all founder applications.
- Shortlist founders.
- Accept exactly 12 founders.
- Reject or defer applicants.
- Review and approve builders.
- Run AI-assisted builder matching.
- Assign builders manually.
- Manage statuses, notes, and progress.

## Selection Model

Founder 12 is selective.

The product should make the selection process visible and serious:

1. Application submitted.
2. Under ISD review.
3. Shortlisted.
4. Accepted to Founder 12.
5. Not selected for this cohort.

Acceptance should unlock cohort-specific surfaces:
- Founders Coffee.
- ISD member privileges.
- AI sessions.
- Team formation support.
- Cohort announcements or external community link.

## Founders Coffee

Founders Coffee is a core part of the accepted-founder experience.

MVP surface:
- Next Founders Coffee date.
- Location or meeting instructions.
- RSVP/status.
- Session theme.
- Prep prompt.
- Host or ISD contact.

Do not build a full calendar system in MVP. A simple accepted-founder module is enough.

## ISD Member Privileges

Accepted founders should understand what being part of ISD unlocks.

MVP surface:
- Benefit title.
- Short description.
- Availability/status.
- Action link or contact instruction.

Examples:
- Founder Coffee access.
- AI sessions.
- ISD community chat.
- Workspace/event access.
- Builder matching support.
- Demo preparation.

Do not build a full membership CRM in MVP.

## AI Sessions

AI sessions are founder support moments offered by ISD/La Mesa.

MVP surface:
- Session title.
- Focus area.
- Date/time or status.
- Prep prompt.
- Access link or admin note.

Examples:
- Product clarity session.
- Prototype roadmap session.
- Pitch preparation session.
- AI workflow session.

Do not build a full LMS or scheduling system in MVP.

## Community Chat

Native chat is out of scope for MVP.

If ISD uses WhatsApp, Slack, Discord, Circle, or another tool, La Mesa can show a gated external link only to accepted founders.

The product should treat chat as a privilege/access link, not as a native feature.

## Team Formation

Team formation happens after acceptance or when ISD decides an applicant is ready for support.

Flow:

1. AI maps roles needed for the venture.
2. ISD reviews the role map.
3. AI suggests possible builders from approved Builder Network profiles.
4. Admin reviews match reasons.
5. Admin manually assigns builders.
6. Founder sees assigned team members and missing roles.

AI recommends. ISD decides.

## Product Tone

Founder 12 should feel:
- Selective
- Warm
- Serious
- Founder-centered
- Costa Rica connected
- ISD-backed
- Practical and execution-oriented

It should not feel:
- Like a public marketplace
- Like a school portal
- Like a generic startup directory
- Like a social feed
- Like a freelance job board

## MVP Decision Rule

If a feature does not help ISD select, coordinate, support, or move the 12 accepted founders toward prototype readiness, defer it.
